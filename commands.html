Commands:

  • /help

    Bot will DM you some help!

  • /say

    Bot will say what you want!

  • /member-count

    Bot will tell you how many members are in the server!

  • /ping

    Bot will tell you it's response time!

  • /server-info

    Bot will tell you some information about the server!

  • /setnick

    Change your nickname in the server!

  • /join-team

    Join a team!

  • /remove-from-team

    Remove someone from your team! (Team Leaders Only)

  • /schedule-embed

    Create an embed for a schedule! (Admins Only)

  • /kick

    Kick someone from the server! (Kick Members Permission Only)

  • /bant

    Ban someone from the server! (Ban Members Permission Only)

  • /unban

    Unban someone from the server!
